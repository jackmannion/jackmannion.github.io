Junior Thesis: "Macroeconomic Consequences of Fiscal Austerity: Evidence From the Alaska Permanent Fund"

»

May 2022

Click this post to see my Junior Thesis. This paper is my first independent research project. I examine the effect of fiscal austerity in Alaska as a result of Alaskan Governor Bill Walker's reduction of the 2016 Alaska Permanent Fund dividend check from $2,000 to $1,000. I find extremely strong negative effects.
