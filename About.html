About Me

My name is Jack Mannion. I am a junior at UC Berkeley majoring in Economics and minoring in Data Science. I particularly enjoy Macroeconomics, Econometrics, and Financial Economics. More recently, since choosing to minor in Data Science, I have grown an interest in coding and creating visualizations of data, especially in the form of economic data.

Why I Built This Website

I built this website for two reasons:

1) To be a way to not only express my thoughts of certain economic, financial, or other topics that may be topical or interesting to me but also to serve as a way to permanently keep a record of my thoughts. I will post these thoughts on my Home page.

2) To serve as a place to put a collection of any projects, either for class or otherwise, that I have done which I feel are intersting enough to warrant a spot online. These projects could range anywhere from as simple as a single graph of data that I find interesting to as complex as an entire research paper (coming soon thanks to Econ 191). I will post these projects on the Projects page.
